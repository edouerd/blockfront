Blockfront is a decentralized intelligence layer for the Ethereum blockchain ecosystem. The Blockfront protocol provides usability and visibility as infrastructure. Blockfront is made for developers, traders, investors, and other network participants. The Blockfront protocol addresses ecosystem intelligence for Ethereum in 3 areas:

  1. Network visibility and ecosystem context
  2. Data accessibility, decentralization, and trust
  3. Query performance and usability

On-demand access to actionable metrics and contextual analysis means Blockfront is the foundation for a new generation of network-driven smart contracts, crypto-products, and dApps. Powered by a resilient and incentive-driven network of data suppliers and validators. All towards the premise of a global supercomputer.
